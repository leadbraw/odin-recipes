Omelette Recipe

It's a classic. Enough said.

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • Some milk
  • Butter (I prefer Kerrigold)
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salt, garlic powder

Steps:

  1. Heat a half inch slab of butter in a pan under medium low heat
  2. While the butter is heating, crack three eggs into a bowl and whisk them with a little milk, salt, and garlic powder.
  3. Once the butter is heated and the pan has coated, pour the eggs in, and add some shredded cheese in a line down the center.
  4. Gently prod the eggs to make sure they arent sticking tto the pan.
  5. Once the whole egg mass is one unit that is sliding around the pan on the butter, flip it in half.
  6. After about 45 seconds, flip it over. Let it finish for another 45 seconds or so, then take it out. You're all done!
