Fried Potatoes Recipe

This is one of my favorite side dishes to make. It pairs nicely with the Shake 'n' Bake Chicken recipe also found on this website.

Ingredients:

  • A potato (preferably a less starchy variety)
  • Some bacon fat
  • Seasonings (garlic powder, salt, pepper, paprika)

Steps:

  1. Heat the bacon fat under medium heat for 5 or so minutes, until it's moving smoothly.
  2. While the bacon fat is heating, slice your potato into slices about a quarter inch thick at maximum.
  3. Put the potatoes in the pan with the bacon fat, add seasonings.
  4. After about 7 or so minutes, flip the potatoes over, add seasonings again.
  5. Once the top side is looking nice and crisp, you're all done! Take them out of the pan.
