Shake 'n' Bake Chicken Recipe

A great dinner option on a budget!

Ingredients:

  • 2 porkchops
  • Pork shake n bake mix in a baggie
  • Baking pan
  • Aluminum foil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Line pan with aluminum foil.
  3. Gently rinse pork chops, put in bag with mix, shake well, then place on pan.
  4. Once oven is done preheating, put chops in for 21 minutes.
  5. After 21 minutes, take the pork chops out. You're all done!
